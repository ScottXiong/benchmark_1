Packaging Process

· Upon receipt of completed packaging form and dielines, Benchmark will prepare and send a quotation that must be signed and returned.

· Once the signed quote is returned to Benchmark, artwork will be routed via ProofHQ, an online proofing system, for supplier review typically within 2-3 business days depending on the quantity and complexity of art files.

· All revision comments should be added in ProofHQ. Typically the supplier will have 2 business days to add feedback.

· Once files are approved by both Walmart Buyer and supplier, Benchmark adds in Spanish translation (if needed) from a Walmart approved translation agency and re-submits translated files to the supplier for final approval.

· Once the translated files are approved by the supplier, final files are released within 1 working day of all proof stakeholders' approval, and Benchmark will archive the final approved files.

· Please note that speed of artwork release depends on accurate proofing and timely stakeholder proof review and approval.
