BW & ONN(发PF,GS确认）

  1. Packaging Specialist (Rosemary) sends completed kickoff form to BMK
  2. BMK reaches out to vendors for the die lines, completed packaging intake forms, and any additional info (2 days). Note that we need to push suppliers to make sure they return the things to us within 2 working days. This is important.
  3. BMK sends completed vendor intake form to PS, CM & GS; BMK prepares and sends quotation to vendors
  4. GS responds back to BMK with any comments or revisions on the completed packaging intake form (3 days); vendor sign cost estimate (2 days)
  5. BMK begins artwork adaptation (10 days)
  6. BMK sends 1st proof to PS, CM, the vendor, and Greg all at the same time (Greg V is a viewer only, not reviewer - others are approvers); BMK gets any feedback (changes required or approved) on 1st proof (2 days)
  7. Approved text from 1st proof is sent for French translation (2-3 days); Photography is completed if applicable (2 days)
  8. 2nd proof (translation/photography finished) is sent to PS, CM & vendor at the same time, for approval (2 days), once approved...
  9. BMK prepares final file(s) and release to vendor (1-2 days)
  10. Vendors are required to send a print samples for colour quality checking (5 days)
